Dark Matter — Official Trailer

Release Date: 2024

Hailed as one of the best sci-fi novels of the decade, Dark Matter
is a story about the road not taken. The series will follow Jason
Dessen (played by Joel Edgerton), a physicist, professor and
family man who — one night while walking home on the streets
of Chicago — is abducted into an alternate version of his life.
Wonder quickly turns o nightmare when he tries to return to his
reality amid the mind-bending landscape of lives he could have
lived. In this labyrinth of realities, he embarks on a harrowing
journey to get back to his true family and save them from the
most terrifying, unbeatable foe imaginable: himself.
