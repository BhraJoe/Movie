Fallout - Official Trailer

Release Date: 7 Mar 2024

Based on one of the greatest video game series of all time,
Fallout is the story of haves and have-nots in a world in which
there’s almost nothing left to have. 200 years after the
apocalypse, the gentle denizens of luxury fallout shelters are
forced to return to the irradiated hellscape their ancestors left
behind — and are shocked to discover an incredibly complex,
gleefully weird and highly violent universe waiting for them. From
executive producers Jonathan Nolan and Lisa Joy, the creators
of Westworld, starring Ella Purnell, Aaron Moten, Walton
Goggins and more. All episodes arrive April 11 on Prime Video.
